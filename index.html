EventLoop

EventLoop is a platform that can identify unique hash tags from users on Instagram. With EventLoop, you can moderate the content posted by users. Any content with a unique hash tags (e.g. #DotRoot) specified for EventLoop can be moderated before they are viewed on screens as the posts are sent to the platform first. The platform, complete with time sequence and looping function, can be used to publicly display photos and posts. This helps in corporate brand posts identification on social media. Time and date of posts can be seen if posts are on livefeed. EventLoop can also be integrated with other Social network accounts such as Facebook and Twitter. EventLoop won third place in BICTA 2015.

Features

  • EventLoop can be used on TV Screens
  • The public can post anything related with Corporate Brands with unique hash tags (#) specified with EventLoop on Instagram
  • Admin can identify all the posts related
  • All posts can be seen on any displays with varying sizes, with customizable time sequence and loop
  • You can customize the background template based on the Event criteria.

How Can EventLoop Help Your Business

EventLoop can provide a 360 degree view of your business or product from customer user experience, users can post on social media and the posts can be advertised to a larger audience. The posts act as digital testimonials and add value to your product.

Food Fuel

FoodFuel is an innovative approach to the traditional meal-planning conundrum. Via FoodFuel, users can order their meal on the go, and accumulate fuel points to fill their ‘FuelBar’ and earn rewards, discounts and many other benefits. FoodFuel won third place in BICTA Awards and the Grand Prize Winner for Ignite Entrepreneurship Challenge in 2013. FoodFuel is available on Google Playstore and Apple Appstore.

Features

  • FoodFuel is an innovative approach to ordering your meals.
  • It allows you to order your meal how you want it, when you want it, where you want it.
  • App allows you to track your orders.
  • Can choose and order mean from your favourite outlets!

Moment

DotRoot is the sole distributor for Moment 3D Printers in Brunei. The printer was presented to His Royal Highness The Crown Prince during The Crown Prince CIPTA Award 2015. DotRoot believes that Brunei is ready to explore 3D Printing especially because Design and Technology has now become a core part of the national curriculum. It is the right time for DotRoot to distribute Moment 3D Printer. Moment 3D Printer is one of the cost-effective and efficient 3D printers in the market.

Features

  • The printer is able to print using plastic filaments which are melted at high temperatures and carefully moulded to the design.
  • It is determined by the user through computer software.
  • It supports either Windows or Mac OS systems
