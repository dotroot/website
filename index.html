EventLoop

Product

EventLoop is a platform that can identify unique has tags from users on Instagram. With EventLoop, you can moderate the content posted by users. Any content with a unique hash tags (e.g. #DotRoot) specified for EventLoop can be moderated before they are viewed on screens as the posts are sent to the platform first. The platformm, complete with time sequence and looping function, can be used to publicly display photos and posts. This helps in corporate brand posts identification on social media. Time and date of posts can be seen if posts are on livefeed. EventLoop can also be integrated with other Social network accounts such as Facebook and Twitter. EventLoop won third place in BICTA 2015.

AMBD Smartphone

Application

This smartphone application aims to, among others, introduce AMBD and financial institutions licensed by AMBD, as well as to provide awareness on financial scams.This app includes info about AMBD, allows users to search an organisation to verify if the organisation is a licensed financial institution, up to date of information on common financial scams in Brunei along with questions to estimate your investment risk, collection of scam awareness videos, list of events by AMBD & function a contact form for any enquiries. We developed the IOS & Android version of the App along with back end Content Mangement System (CMS) to update data in the AMBD Application.

ALAFRun FotoVent

Application

ALAFRun FotoVent is a platform that organizers can use to take photos of attendees/participants and upload it to our secure "cloud" with their tags. They have the option to watermark/frame the photo with any watermark/frame that they want. Attendees/participants can grab their photo through the platform by first logging in and then entering the details of the event and their tag. The photos taken by the event organizers will then be shared to the attendees'/participants' account.

About us

Dotroot Technologies

Who We Are

DotRoot Technologies is a solutions centre that designs, develops and implements mobile solution. The company also offers wide range of multimedia solution such as Graphic Designs, Video editing for Corporate Advertisement and, Events and Web Development. DotRoot's expertise in Mobile Application Development is versatile with the drive to strive and to innovate for success locally and internationally. DotRoot has developed applications for a vast range of clients and numerous multimedia projects for both private and public sector organisations.

Vision

Our product portfolio is built around the concept of globalizing local talents. They are meant to push the envelope in terms of what local Bruneian IT companies can do. We hope to contribute to the development of Brunei Darussalam through the internationalization of its IT industry. We hope to attract international interest and to be a bridge between the local IT industry and those outside.
